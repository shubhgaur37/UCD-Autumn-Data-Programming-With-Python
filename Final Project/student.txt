# Attributes for both male_stud.csv and female_stud.csv datasets:
1 large_fam - family size (binary: "0" - less or equal to 3 or "1" - greater than 3)
2 lives_in_city - student's residence type (binary: "1" - urban area or "0" - rural area)
3 traveltime - home to school travel time (numeric: 1 - <15 min., 2 - 15 to 30 min., 3 - 30 min. to 1 hour, or 4 - >1 hour)
4 studytime - weekly study time (numeric: 1 - <2 hours, 2 - 2 to 5 hours, 3 - 5 to 10 hours, or 4 - >10 hours)
5 failures - number of past class failures (numeric: n if 1<=n<3, else 4)
6 paid - extra paid classes within the course subject (binary: yes or no)
7 activities - extra-curricular activities (binary: yes or no)
8 internet - Internet access at home (binary: yes or no)
9 romantic - with a romantic relationship (binary: yes or no)
10 famrel - quality of family relationships (numeric: from 1 - very bad to 5 - excellent)
11 freetime - free time after school (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
12 goout - going out with friends (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
13 absences - number of school absences (numeric: from 0 to 93)
14 final_grade (numeric: from 0 to 20, output target)
